Who we are

DomeTV is an American media group focused on everything from public policy to casual political discussions. Founded by a group of motivated high school students, DomeTV offers the latest policy insights and perspectives on different issues that our nation faces in the 21st century. We seek to use our content to influence the old and new generations of Americans in order to have a more knowledgable and informed American population. Our values are staunchly based on the principles of free markets and liberty.
